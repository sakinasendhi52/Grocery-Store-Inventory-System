About Larder & Co.

A shelf built one maker at a time.

We started in a single farmers market stall in 2014, selling honey from the hives behind the founder's house. Today we work with a small, growing roster of independent makers no distributors, no anonymous supply chains. Every listing is written by the person who actually made the thing and every order ships straight from their kitchen, grove or dairy to your door.

If it's on our shelf, we've tasted it, asked about how it's made and would happily serve it at our own table.
